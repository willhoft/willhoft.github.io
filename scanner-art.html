Scanner Art

The images on this page were created using a scanner. The items were placed directly on the scanner glass. The light comes basically only from the scanner and some of the items don't reflect the light back into the scanner, so some of the objects take on a look that is very different from what we normally see. Some of the objects were stationary while scanned. However, some were moved during the scanning, with very interesting results.
